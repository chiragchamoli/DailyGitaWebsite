Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Daily Gita ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Daily Gita mobile application, website, and related services (the "App"). We designed Daily Gita to work with minimal personal information and to keep your spiritual practice private.

1. Information We Collect

Daily Gita does not require you to create an account. Depending on how you use the App, we may process:

  • App preferences, such as your chosen language, theme, notification timing, and other settings used to personalise your experience.
  • Saved content, such as shlokas, reflections, or cards you choose to save in the App.
  • Notification information, such as the technical token needed to send notifications if you choose to enable them.
  • Basic app and device data, such as app version, device type, crash reports, and anonymous or aggregated usage information that helps us improve Daily Gita.
  • Messages you send us, including your email address and the contents of your message if you contact us for support or feedback.

We do not sell your personal information.

2. How We Use Information

  • To deliver daily shlokas, translations, reflections, widgets, and reminders.
  • To remember your preferences and saved content.
  • To respond to support requests and feedback.
  • To monitor reliability, fix bugs, and improve the App.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect the App from misuse.

3. Notifications

If you enable notifications, we use your device's push notification system to deliver reminders and daily content at the frequency you choose. You can adjust or turn off notifications at any time in the App or in your device settings.

4. Data Storage & Security

Preferences and saved content are stored primarily on your device. Some technical information may be processed by service providers that help us operate the App. We use reasonable safeguards to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

5. Third-Party Services

Daily Gita may rely on third-party services such as app stores, analytics, crash reporting, hosting, or notification providers. These services process information under their own privacy policies and terms.

6. Children's Privacy

Daily Gita is not directed to children under 13 or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of personal information we hold about you. To make a request, contact us using the email below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above. If changes are material, we may provide additional notice where appropriate.

9. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at nikita@dailygita.app.
